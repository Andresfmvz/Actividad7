Law On Time

Law On Time is a conglomerate of volunteering lawyers with a strong sense of justice and social equality. Our mission is to help those who cannot defend themselves and have suffered from unfair treatment by big corporations or even federal institutions. Historically, lawyers picked up cases based on personal gain and completely forgot about their role in society, turning the judicial system into a profitable business. Our firm prioritizes justice and resolution, and our lawyers are willing to go the extra mile to achieve success.

We all remember the cases from 2017 in which corrupted judges made unfair decisions based on their own personal experiences instead of the evidence, these were national news as those actions revived the old struggles not just by minorities, but by common citizens to be heard and to be fairly treated

We are present in almost every area of the judicial system, and we take action fast. As soon as the Victim Form is filled and delivered our professionals start studying the case. The following are the most common services (click on the items below):

  • Heritage and family state.

  • Sexual harassment (general).

  • Unjustified job loss.

  • Fraud.

  • Abuse of power.
