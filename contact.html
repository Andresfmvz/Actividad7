Law On Time

By filling the form and clicking on the button “send” you agree to let us use that information to get in contact with you, and to start building the case. Please keep in mind that we will reply to your request in the next 24 hours, so be patient, and if you are in an emergency situation, get in contact the local authorities.

The first meeting is always free as it is determined whether opening a legal case is necessary or not. From there, each meeting must be paid in advance with a cost of $40 (US). Usually it will take two or three more meetings before you get assigned to the proper professional which has his or her own hourly fee (usually between $50 to $60 per hour). It is common for our lawyers to demand to the other party compensation for the expenses of your case.
